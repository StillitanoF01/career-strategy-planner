## Project description
**Statement:** A commercial building organised around water catchment and treatment, adaptively reusing an existing structure on a flood-prone site in Waterloo.
**Brief:** The brief called for the adaptive reuse of an existing commercial building, with water management as the main focus.
**Design intent:** A resolved multi-use precinct.
**Key challenges:** Adaptive reuse of a heritage structure.
**Final outcome:** A scheme that registers the degrading site over time, driven by Waterloo's flooding API data.
