## Project description
**Statement:** A reuse housing project centred on an existing workers cottage in Redfern.
**Brief:** The brief asked for the adaptive reuse of a workers cottage, balancing heritage retention with new household needs.
**Design intent:** Working closely with the neighbouring context, the design extends the cottage without displacing its relationship to the street and adjoining properties.
**Key challenges:** The site's small footprint constrained the scope for new construction and pushed the project toward careful, incremental intervention rather than wholesale rebuilding.
**Final outcome:** A sustainability-led response that prioritised retaining existing fabric and improving the building's environmental performance within the site's tight constraints.
